About Us

Meet the force behind the brand – a concise overview of our ethos and commitment to excellence.

4P’s is started with a mission to transform the unorganized real estate sector and help Companies, Brands, Retailers and Individuals with the perfect opportunities.

4P’s Realty Services stands as a testament to expertise and vision, founded by Jhansi Desavath, a distinguished Post Graduate from IIM Lucknow. With a decade-long career in renowned corporations like Bharti Airtel, Park Plus, and Disney Star, Jhansi brings a wealth of experience in key areas such as Client Relationship Management, Business Development, and Channel Partner Management.

During her tenure, she played pivotal roles in shaping successful strategies. For Park Plus, she fostered strategic alliances with prominent Malls in South, enhancing the brand's presence and reach. At Disney Star, her focus on driving Pricing Strategy showcased her analytical acumen and business foresight. These roles not only highlight her diverse skill set but also underscore her ability to navigate complex corporate landscapes.

With 4P’s Realty Services, Jhansi leverages her extensive corporate experience to prioritize the nuanced requirements of businesses. Her approach is not just about transactions; it's about fostering relationships, understanding the pulse of the market, and aligning real estate opportunities with business goals. By seamlessly integrating her corporate insights into the real estate domain, she ensures that every client receives tailored solutions that go beyond expectations.
